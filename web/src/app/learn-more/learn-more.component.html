Who is Calipr for?

You spend hours in Photoshop creating the perfect design but the website never quite matches. You spend a week working with your developers trying to clean up the last details. A week later everything is off again.

Calipr was built for you.


Step 1: Register your website

Simply entery the base URL to your website. We find all the pages from here.

Step 2: Choose which browsers you want to check

Select the browsers you want to watch for. We support the following options:

  • Chrome
  • iPhone
  • iPad
  • Android

Every night we look at your website to find any changes. When a change is detected we send an email notification.

Step 3: Approve or Reject the changes

When a change is detected you will be prompted with a screen showing the difference in red. If the change is correct simply approve. If the change is incorrect, reject and your developers will receive an email with the relevant information.
